# Exec Plan

## Goal

Complete the remaining Period 4 SaaS polish surfaces after the tracker story.

## Scope

In scope:

- Public landing page.
- Pricing placeholder proof.
- Protected settings page with live workspace data.
- Dashboard/demo labels reflecting implemented modules.
- Unit/static tests, browser proof, and build proof.

Out of scope:

- Payment processing.
- Destructive data deletion.
- New data model or migration.
- External provider integration.

## Risk Classification

Risk flags:

- Public contracts.
- Existing behavior.
- Weak proof.
- Multi-domain.

Hard gates:

- None.

## Work Phases

1. Discovery.
2. Design.
3. Validation planning.
4. Implementation.
5. Verification.
6. Harness update.

## Stop Conditions

Pause for human confirmation if:

- The pricing placeholder needs to collect payment details.
- Settings must perform destructive mutations.
- Public route protection needs to change.
